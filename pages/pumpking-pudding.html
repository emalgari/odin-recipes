Pumpkin Pudding

Ingredients

  • Pumpkin 1Medium sized
  • Milk 1Liter
  • Salt 1tsp
  • Sugar 2tbs
  • Water 0.25Liter

Method

  1. Set a large pot of water to boil.

  2. Peal and chop the pumpkin into cubes.

  3. When water is boiling then add the pumpkin cubes to the pot and boil for 5 minutes, or until softened.

  4. Strain water from the pumpkin and puree the cooked pumpkin.

  5. Add milk to the pumpkin and blend together.

  6. Add salt and sugar, then boil for 30 minutes and stirring frequently.

  7. Allow to cool slightly and then serve into bowls.
