Takhari Rice Pudding

Ingredients

  • Milk ½ Liter
  • Rice 450g
  • Salt 1tsp
  • Sugar 200g
  • Water 1Liter
  • Butter/Oil 20g

Method

  1. Wash the rice, add it in pre-boiled water with the milk and boil it for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  2. Add salt and let it cook.

  3. Serve the rice on serving platter or in a large boil.

  4. Lastly, add heated butter and spread sugar over the butter on the top of the rice. Optionally,top with cinnamon, cardamon and/or pistachio, to taste.
