Haleem

Ingredients

Items Quantity
White wheat 440g
Rice 220g
Chicken breast -
Oil 0.250g
Sugar 105g
Ground dry cardamon 1 tsp
Ground cinnamon 1 tsp
Salt 1tsp
Water 2 Liters

Direction:


  1. Soak the rice in water for 2 hours.

  2. Boil the chicken in water until tenderized, then shred it.

  3. Boil the wheat in 1 liter of water, then blend it along with the soaked rice in a blender.

  4. Add the mixture into a pot over medium heat. Once heated, turn down to medium heat.

  5. Slowly add the remaining water and salt to the pot until the Ingredients look like a soft dough mixture.

  6. Add the shredded chicken into the pot and heat under medium heat for 10 minutes.

  7. Fry the Haleem mixture in the oil, the serve on a serving platter. Spread the sugar and cinnamon over before serving.
