Sos Abba (Tumeric Sauce)

Ingredients

  • Oil 175ml
  • Flour 300g
  • Water 1Liter
  • Black pepper ½ tsp
  • Tumeric powder ½ tsp

Method

  1. Heat the oil in a pot on medium heat.

  2. Add flour to the hot oil and fry it. Keep stirring constantly, while frying to avoid burning the flour.

  3. Add water, tumeric powder and black pepper to the flour and let it cook for ½ hour.

  4. The dish is ready to serve. May be served as a sauce spooned over rice or over the meat.
